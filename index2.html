Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil.
  2. Cook spaghetti until al dente; drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook pancetta or guanciale until crispy.
  4. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper.
  5. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta, then pour the egg mixture over it.
  6. Toss quickly and serve hot.
